§ Disclaimers

The content presented here — including constructs, measurement instruments, behaviors/tasks, and the relationships among them — is derived from automatically extracting implied definitions and connections from a corpus of academic papers spanning ICLR 2024, NeurIPS 2024, ICLR 2025, NAACL 2025, ACL 2025, ICML 2025, and EMNLP 2025, primarily using Large Language Models (LLMs) w/ human expert validation.

Please Note:
  • Indicative Only: All content is indicative, not definitive. It is intended to reflect the current landscape of agreement and disagreement within the field, not to serve as a verified source of truth.
  • No Endorsement: The inclusion of any definition or connection does not constitute an endorsement by the developers.
  • LLM Extraction: As LLMs are used for extraction, the content may contain errors, omissions, or misclassifications.
Our position.

We emphasize that relationships between constructs, measurements, behaviors, and tasks should not be imported wholesale from theories of human intelligence, but instead derived through careful empirical study, hypothesis formation, and hypothesis testing in AI systems. The nomological network presented here does not reflect such validation; rather, it summarizes how the field has implicitly operationalized these relationships in recent work.
