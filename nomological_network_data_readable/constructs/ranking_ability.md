# Ranking ability
**Type:** Construct  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  
**Also known as:** Ranking accuracy  

## State of the Field

The latent ability to correctly order multiple responses based on their relative quality (e.g., faithfulness or correctness) rather than assigning absolute scores.
