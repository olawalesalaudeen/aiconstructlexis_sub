# Interlocutor awareness
**Type:** Construct  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  

## State of the Field

The latent ability of an LLM to infer the identity and characteristics of its conversational partner, enabling adaptive behavior based on the interlocutor's model family, reasoning patterns, linguistic style, or alignment preferences.
