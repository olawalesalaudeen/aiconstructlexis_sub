# Preference bias
**Type:** Construct  
**Referenced in:** 3 papers  
**Also known as:** Human preference bias  

## State of the Field

The inherent tendency of a large language model to favor certain outputs, such as specific conversational strategies, over other equally or more appropriate alternatives.
