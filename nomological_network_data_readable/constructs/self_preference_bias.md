# Self-preference bias
**Type:** Construct  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  
**Also known as:** Self-enhancement bias  

## State of the Field

A tendency for an evaluator to favor outputs from models in the same family or with similar training patterns.

## Sources

**[An Empirical Analysis of Uncertainty in Large Language Model Evaluations](https://proceedings.iclr.cc/paper_files/paper/2025/file/82d3258eb58ceac31744a88005b7ddef-Paper-Conference.pdf)**
> A tendency for an evaluator to favor outputs from models in the same family or with similar training patterns.

**[Justice or Prejudice? Quantifying Biases in LLM-as-a-Judge](https://proceedings.iclr.cc/paper_files/paper/2025/file/fdca08d371e4b6c031397909e20043bd-Paper-Conference.pdf) (as "Self-enhancement bias")**
> A tendency for an LLM judge to favor responses generated by itself over responses generated by other models.
