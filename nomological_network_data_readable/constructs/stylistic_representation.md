# Stylistic representation
**Type:** Construct  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  
**Also known as:** Literary style encoding  

## State of the Field

The latent ability of a language model to encode abstract, author-specific patterns such as syntax, diction, and rhetorical structure from text, independent of semantic or factual content.
