# Textual aesthetics
**Type:** Construct  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  
**Also known as:** Layout, Design quality  

## State of the Field

The latent trait encompassing the aesthetic quality of text generated by LLMs, including readability, visual organization, consistency of style, and logical coherence, which contributes to user engagement and comprehension.
