# Racial and ethnic bias
**Type:** Construct  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  
**Also known as:** Ethnicity bias, Racial bias  

## State of the Field

The latent tendency of a model to systematically produce different clinical decisions or explanations based on patient ethnicity, reflecting societal or training data disparities.
