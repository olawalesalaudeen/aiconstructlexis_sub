# Implicit personalization
**Type:** Construct  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  
**Also known as:** Stereotype susceptibility  

## State of the Field

The latent tendency of an LLM to infer and act upon user demographic attributes based on subtle conversational cues, even when not explicitly stated, thereby tailoring responses in ways that may reflect stereotypes.
