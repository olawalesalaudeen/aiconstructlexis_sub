# Pedagogical ability
**Type:** Construct  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  
**Also known as:** Language teaching ability  

## State of the Field

The latent capacity of a model to guide student learning through effective teaching strategies such as scaffolding, questioning, and error diagnosis, rather than simply providing answers.
