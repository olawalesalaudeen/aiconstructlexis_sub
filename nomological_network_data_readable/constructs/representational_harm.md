# Representational harm
**Type:** Construct  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  

## State of the Field

The latent property of an LLM’s outputs that misrepresent or marginalize certain nations by limiting their visibility or associating them with negative traits, thereby reinforcing global inequities.
