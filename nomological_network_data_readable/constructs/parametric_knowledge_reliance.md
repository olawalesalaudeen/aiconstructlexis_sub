# Parametric knowledge reliance
**Type:** Construct  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  
**Also known as:** Context unfaithfulness  

## State of the Field

The tendency of LLMs to rely on parametric knowledge rather than faithfully reasoning over provided contextual information, especially when the two conflict.
