# Fine-grained video understanding
**Type:** Construct  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  
**Also known as:** Fine-level video understanding  

## State of the Field

The ability of a model to comprehend and reason about specific, detailed events and their temporal relationships within a video, as opposed to just understanding the overall gist.
