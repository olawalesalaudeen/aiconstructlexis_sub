# Nationality bias
**Type:** Construct  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  

## State of the Field

A systematic and unfair tendency of a model to generate disproportionately positive or negative sentiments towards specific countries or regions, or to unfairly center or erase certain nations in its generations.
