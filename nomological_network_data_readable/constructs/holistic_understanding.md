# Holistic understanding
**Type:** Construct  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  
**Also known as:** Holistic object understanding  

## State of the Field

The latent ability of a model to grasp the overall scene, context, and structure of an image, as opposed to just identifying individual objects.
