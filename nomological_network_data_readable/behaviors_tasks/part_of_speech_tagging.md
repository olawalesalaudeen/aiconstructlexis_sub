# Part-of-speech tagging
**Type:** Behavior  
**Referenced in:** 5 papers  

## State of the Field

Based on the provided data, part-of-speech tagging is defined as the task of assigning a part of speech to a word within a text. This assignment is based on both the word's individual definition and its surrounding context. The single source paper frames this as one of several "traditional NLP tasks". In terms of operationalization, the LinCE benchmark is mentioned as an instrument that addresses part-of-speech tagging, which is also referred to by the abbreviation POS.
