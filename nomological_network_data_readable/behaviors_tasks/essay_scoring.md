# Essay scoring
**Type:** Behavior  
**Referenced in:** 4 papers  

## State of the Field

Based on the provided literature, essay scoring is characterized as a task of automatic evaluation. It is defined as the process of automatically assessing the writing skills of a language learner, specifically as those skills are demonstrated in a text they have produced. This task is also referred to as automated essay scoring (AES). The central goal, as described in the source material, is to evaluate a learner's writing abilities based on their written output.
