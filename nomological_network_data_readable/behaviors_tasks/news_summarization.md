# News summarization
**Type:** Behavior  
**Referenced in:** 3 papers  

## State of the Field

Based on the provided data, news summarization is defined as the task of generating a concise summary of a news article. It is framed as a generative task, with at least one study evaluating the zero-shot generative performance of models on this behavior. To operationalize and measure this capability, the literature in this set uses the CNN/DM dataset. The focus is on a model's ability to produce a condensed version of a given news text without specific fine-tuning for the task.
