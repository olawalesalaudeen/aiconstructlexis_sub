# Post-hoc rationalization
**Type:** Behavior  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  
**Also known as:** Post-hoc explanation, Post-hoc rationalisation  

## State of the Field

The generation of a Chain-of-Thought that justifies a predetermined answer without substantially influencing it, often indicated by flat confidence trajectories.
