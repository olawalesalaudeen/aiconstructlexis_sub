# MedHallu
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  

## State of the Field

A benchmark of 10,000 medical question-answer pairs with systematically generated hallucinated answers, stratified by difficulty (easy, medium, hard) and hallucination type, used to evaluate medical hallucination detection in LLMs.
