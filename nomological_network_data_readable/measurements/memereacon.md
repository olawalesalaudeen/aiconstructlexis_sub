# MemeReaCon
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  

## State of the Field

A benchmark for evaluating contextual meme understanding in vision-language models, consisting of 1,565 annotated Reddit posts with meme images, post text, and top comments, assessed across four tasks probing interplay, stance, connections, and intent.
