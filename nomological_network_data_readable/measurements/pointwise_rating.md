# Pointwise rating
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  
**Also known as:** Score-based evaluation  

## State of the Field

An evaluation method where an LLM is prompted to assign a numerical score to individual answers based on their correctness, which are then compared to establish a preference.
