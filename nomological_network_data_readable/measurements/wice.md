# WiCE
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  
**Also known as:** WICE  

## State of the Field

Textual entailment dataset based on Wikipedia claims, used in this work for connected reasoning evaluation due to its document-claim-evidence triples.

## Sources

> Dataset of natural claims extracted from Wikipedia, used to evaluate fact-checking performance at the claim level with binary supported/unsupported labels.
