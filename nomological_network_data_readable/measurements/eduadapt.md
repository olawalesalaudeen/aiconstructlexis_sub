# EDUADAPT
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  

## State of the Field

A benchmark dataset of nearly 48,000 grade-labeled question-answer pairs across nine science subjects for Grades 1–12, designed to evaluate grade-level adaptability in LLMs through both multiple-choice and open-ended questions.
