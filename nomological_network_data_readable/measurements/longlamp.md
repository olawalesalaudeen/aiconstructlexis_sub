# LongLaMP
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 4 papers  

## State of the Field

Across the provided sources, LongLaMP is consistently defined as a benchmark and dataset for evaluating long-form personalized text generation. It is framed as an extended version of the LaMP benchmark, with a specific focus on tasks that are longer and more subjective. The papers use it to assess performance on several open-ended generation tasks, including abstract generation, topic writing, and review generation. One source further specifies that the "tasks in LongLaMP tend to involve more subjective or user-specific expression" (Uncovering the Bigger Picture). In practice, it is adopted alongside the LaMP benchmark to provide evaluation across both short-form and long-form personalized text generation.
