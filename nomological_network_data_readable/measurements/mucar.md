# MUCAR
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  

## State of the Field

A benchmark for evaluating multilingual cross-modal ambiguity resolution, consisting of 1278 samples in Chinese, English, and Malay, including dual-ambiguity cases where both text and image are ambiguous but jointly resolve to a single interpretation.
