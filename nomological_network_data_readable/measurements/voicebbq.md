# VoiceBBQ
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  

## State of the Field

A spoken-language extension of the BBQ benchmark that assesses social bias in spoken language models using synthesized speech contexts with controlled speaker gender and accent, enabling separate evaluation of content and acoustic influences on bias.
