# FineWeb-Edu
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 6 papers  
**Also known as:** Fineweb-Edu  

## State of the Field

Across the provided sources, FineWeb-Edu is characterized as a large, curated dataset of high-quality educational text for language models. The dataset is derived from CommonCrawl and is identified as containing English text. The curation process is described as involving LLM-based annotation to assess the "educational value" of documents. Its scale is represented differently across papers, with one source citing "10 billion tokens" and another mentioning "approximately 450,000" annotated documents. The documented uses for FineWeb-Edu include training language models and serving as a source for data sampling, while one definition also notes its role in evaluation.
