# PathVQA
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 7 papers  
**Also known as:** PATHVQA  

## State of the Field

Across the provided literature, PathVQA is consistently characterized as a benchmark dataset for Visual Question Answering (VQA) within the medical domain. The definitions uniformly describe its focus as pathology, using images from sources like textbooks and online libraries to test a model's ability to interpret histopathology slides. As a measurement instrument, papers use PathVQA to evaluate model performance on the behavior of "Medical visual question answering" and the broader capability of "Visual question answering". The dataset is frequently employed in evaluations alongside other biomedical VQA benchmarks, such as VQA-RAD and SLAKE. One study notes its use in assessing the processing of "text reports and medical images in the form of PATHVQA for pathology" ("HEMM: Holistic Evaluation of Multimodal Foundation Models").
