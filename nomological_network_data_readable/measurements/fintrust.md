# FINTRUST
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  

## State of the Field

A comprehensive benchmark with 15,680 question-answer pairs across textual, tabular, and time-series data, designed to evaluate LLM trustworthiness in finance through seven dimensions: truthfulness, safety, fairness, robustness, privacy, transparency, and knowledge discovery.
