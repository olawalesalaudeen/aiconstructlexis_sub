# MD-judge
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 3 papers  

## State of the Field

Based on the available data, MD-judge is an automated evaluator designed to assess how LLM-generated responses handle specific ethical considerations. The single provided definition characterizes it as a tool to evaluate whether responses appropriately address fairness and privacy concerns, specifically in terms of their safety and appropriateness. In its operationalization, MD-judge is used to measure the construct of "Privacy awareness". The paper "DebateCoder: Towards Collective Intelligence ofLLMs via Test Case DrivenLLMDebate for Code Generation" employs it for this purpose, stating its role is "to assess the LLM’s generated responses regarding Qf and Qp".
