# WikiDatacounterfact
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 4 papers  

## State of the Field

WikiDatacounterfact is characterized in the provided literature as a dataset used for knowledge editing evaluation. The single available definition states that it contains "counterfactual statements derived from Wikidata." Its most common application is to measure the construct of "Knowledge editing," with some papers specifying its use in experiments on "knowledge insertion and knowledge modification." A less frequent use is also noted, where one paper employs it to measure "Locality." One source also situates the dataset within the broader "KnowEdit (Zhang et al., 2024) benchmark."
