# Asch conformity experiment
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  
**Also known as:** Asch conformity experiments  

## State of the Field

A series of classic social psychology experiments studying how individual judgment is influenced by a majority group opinion, which inspired the design of the Trust and Doubt protocols.
