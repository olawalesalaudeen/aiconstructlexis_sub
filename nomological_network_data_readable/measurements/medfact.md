# MEDFACT
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  

## State of the Field

A large-scale Chinese dataset for evidence-based medical fact-checking of LLM-generated claims, containing 1,321 questions and 7,409 claim-evidence pairs with veracity labels across five categories: Supported, Partially Supported, Refuted, Uncertain, and Not Applicable.
