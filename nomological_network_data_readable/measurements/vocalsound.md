# VocalSound
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 4 papers  

## State of the Field

Across the provided sources, VocalSound is consistently characterized as a dataset for Vocal Sound Classification (VSC). It is used to evaluate a model's ability to recognize non-speech audio events, with a specific focus on what one paper calls "non-verbal human vocalizations." The dataset is described as featuring these sounds across "different acoustic conditions." The stated purpose of this instrument is to challenge models to recognize human vocal sounds beyond the domain of speech.
