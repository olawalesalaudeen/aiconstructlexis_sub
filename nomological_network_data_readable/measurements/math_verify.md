# Math-Verify
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 4 papers  

## State of the Field

Within the provided literature, Math-Verify is characterized as a tool for automated assessment. It is defined as a reproducible procedure designed to programmatically verify the correctness of generated mathematical solutions. The sole source paper describes its application as a filtering mechanism in research design. Specifically, it is used to select problems that are computationally solvable and whose solutions are verifiable by the tool itself, distinguishing them from problems that might require proof-based reasoning.
