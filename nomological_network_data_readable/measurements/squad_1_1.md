# SQuAD 1.1
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 5 papers  
**Also known as:** SQuAD v1  

## State of the Field

Across the provided literature, SQuAD 1.1 is consistently defined as a reading comprehension benchmark. The definitions are in agreement, describing it as a dataset based on Wikipedia articles where the answer to every question must be a direct text span from the provided passage. The primary and explicit use of this instrument is to measure the behavior of "Question answering." Evidence from multiple papers shows it is used for "QA tasks," with one source specifying its questions as "single-hop." The instrument is referred to as both "SQuAD v1.1" and "SQuAD v1" in the source materials, though the underlying description of its structure and purpose remains consistent.
