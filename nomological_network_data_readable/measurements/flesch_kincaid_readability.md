# Flesch-Kincaid readability
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  
**Also known as:** Flesch-Kincaid Grade Score, Flesch–Kincaid Readability score  

## State of the Field

Readability metric that estimates the U.S. school grade level required to understand a text, used to assess the comprehension difficulty of questions and answers.
