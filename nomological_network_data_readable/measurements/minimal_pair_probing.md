# Minimal pair probing
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  
**Also known as:** Minimal-pair probing framework  

## State of the Field

An evaluation procedure where a simple classifier is trained on a model's internal representations to distinguish between minimally different pairs of inputs (e.g., grammatical vs. ungrammatical sentences) to diagnose the presence of specific encoded knowledge.
