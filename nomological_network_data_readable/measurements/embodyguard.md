# EMBODYGUARD
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  

## State of the Field

A PDDL-grounded benchmark containing 942 LLM-generated scenarios for evaluating physical safety in embodied decision-making, split into EMBODYGUARDmal (malicious commands) and EMBODYGUARDsit (situational hazards).
