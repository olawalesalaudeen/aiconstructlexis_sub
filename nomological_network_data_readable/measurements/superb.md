**Type:** Measurement

## State of the Field

Across the provided sources, SUPERB is characterized as a benchmark designed to measure spoken language understanding. The instrument is specifically described as focusing on "coarse-grained semantic understanding tasks." One paper offers a critique, stating that the benchmark "overlook[s] the importance of various acoustic features." In addition to its use in evaluating speech understanding, SUPERB is also studied in relation to speech recognition.
