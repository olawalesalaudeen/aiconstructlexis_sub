# Weibo21
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 5 papers  

## State of the Field

Across the provided literature, Weibo21 is consistently used as a Chinese-language benchmark dataset for evaluating models on tasks related to identifying false information. The specific capability it is stated to measure is variously described as 'fake news detection', 'rumor detection', and 'misinformation detection'. The dataset is sourced from the Weibo social media platform, with one paper specifying that it is a multi-domain benchmark covering 9 domains where each news item includes both content and comments. In practice, researchers use Weibo21 to evaluate models, sometimes reporting metrics like the F1 score, and it appears in studies alongside other datasets for similar tasks, such as GossipCop and AMTCele.
