## State of the Field

CSCD-NS is characterized in the provided literature as a benchmark dataset for Chinese Spelling Correction (CSC). The single available definition describes it as a "high-quality" dataset where the errors are specifically designed to stem from pinyin input methods, featuring a high number of "homophonic and word-level errors." One source positions it as a "new" benchmark intended to address the limitations of prior datasets like SIGHAN. In terms of its application, the provided data indicates that CSCD-NS is used as a measurement instrument for "Evaluation."
