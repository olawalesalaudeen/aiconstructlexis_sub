# MSVD
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 7 papers  

## State of the Field

Across the provided sources, MSVD is consistently described as a video understanding benchmark. It is specifically used to assess "short video comprehension" and is listed among other "video-based visual-language benchmarks." The benchmark is operationalized to evaluate several multimodal tasks. For instance, one source defines its use for measuring "multimodal video question answering or captioning performance." Another paper further specifies its application in this area, categorizing it as one of the "open-ended VideoQA datasets" for model evaluation. The collective data thus positions MSVD as an instrument for measuring a model's ability to comprehend and respond to queries about short video content.
