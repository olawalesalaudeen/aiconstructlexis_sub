## State of the Field

The Contract Understanding Atticus Dataset (CUAD) is defined as a corpus of commercial legal contracts. According to the provided data, its stated purpose is for evaluating automated contract review and provision extraction. Beyond this specific application, the dataset is also operationalized as a measurement instrument for broader constructs. Specifically, the literature in this set uses CUAD to measure both `Natural language understanding` and `Faithfulness`. This positions the dataset as a tool for assessing how well models can comprehend and accurately represent information within the specialized domain of legal documents.
