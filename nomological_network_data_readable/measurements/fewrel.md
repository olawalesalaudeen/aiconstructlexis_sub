# FewRel
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 5 papers  

## State of the Field

Across the provided literature, FewRel is predominantly characterized as a benchmark dataset for few-shot relation extraction, used to evaluate model performance in low-resource or continual learning settings. This prevailing usage is seen in papers that employ it for Few-shot Continual Relation Extraction (FCRE) tasks, often conducting experiments alongside the TACRED benchmark. A less common framing, presented in one study, describes FewRel as a dataset for type discovery, specifically for "identifying relation types from text". This work also notes that the dataset originated from the field of information extraction, a behavior it is reported to measure. In practice, researchers use FewRel to compare their methods against baselines and evaluate models such as BERT and LLM2Vec.
