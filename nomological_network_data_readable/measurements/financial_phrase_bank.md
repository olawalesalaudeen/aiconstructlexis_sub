# Financial Phrase Bank
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 3 papers  
**Also known as:** FPB  

## State of the Field

Across the provided literature, Financial Phrase Bank is consistently described as a dataset used for financial sentiment analysis. It is composed of sentences derived from financial news and, as one paper adds, financial statements. The primary use of this dataset is to evaluate and measure sentiment classification, a task defined as extracting sentiment information from financial texts. There is general agreement on its purpose, though the specific sentiment labels are described with some variation. While most sources refer to three categories (positive, negative, or neutral), one snippet states that the "classification is positive or negative." Overall, the Financial Phrase Bank is presented as an instrument to operationalize the task of sentiment analysis on financial texts.
