# MORBENCH
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  
**Also known as:** Multilingual over-refusal benchmark  

## State of the Field

Multilingual Over-Refusal Benchmark, a large-scale, automatically constructed evaluation set designed to assess overrefusal behaviors in LLMs across 7 languages and 12 safety categories by targeting prompts near the safety decision boundary.
