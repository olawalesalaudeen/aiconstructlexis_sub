# SentEval
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 4 papers  

## State of the Field

Based on the available data, SentEval is characterized as a standard evaluation suite used in the context of transfer learning. It is specifically positioned to evaluate sentence embeddings. The suite is operationalized through a collection of downstream tasks. These tasks, as described in one source, include sentiment classification (using datasets like MR and SST-2), subjectivity detection (SUBJ), and question classification (TREC). The same source confirms its application by stating its use of specific tasks from the suite: "MR, CR, SUBJ, MPQA, SST-2, TREC, and MRPC" (Refuse Whenever You Feel Unsafe: Improving Safety inLLMs via Decoupled Refusal Training).
