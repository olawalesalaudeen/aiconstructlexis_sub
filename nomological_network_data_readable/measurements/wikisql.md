# WikiSQL
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 7 papers  

## State of the Field

Across the provided sources, WikiSQL is consistently characterized as a benchmark dataset for text-to-SQL tasks. The prevailing definition describes it as a "standard text-to-SQL dataset," with one paper specifying its focus on parsing over single relational tables. A less common framing characterizes it as a benchmark for evaluating model performance on "structured knowledge grounding tasks." The primary use of WikiSQL is for evaluation, as it is shown in tables comparing model results against baselines. In terms of what it assesses, one paper reports that WikiSQL is used to measure the construct of "Table understanding." The dataset is also mentioned as a corpus to which new methodologies can be adapted and is situated alongside other benchmarks like Spider.
