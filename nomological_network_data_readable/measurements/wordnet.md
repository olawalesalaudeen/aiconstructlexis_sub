# WordNet
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 6 papers  

## State of the Field

Within the provided literature, WordNet is characterized as a lexical database that functions as a sense inventory. Its specified application is for Word Sense Disambiguation (WSD) training and evaluation. The source paper describes it as "the most widely adopted resource in WSD research." Structurally, the database is composed of what are described as "lemma-pos-definition triples" to represent distinct sense entries. The provided data also specifies its scale, noting that it contains "147,306 lemmas and 206,941 distinct sense entries."
