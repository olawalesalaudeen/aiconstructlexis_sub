# CultSportQA
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  
**Also known as:** Lets Play Across Cultures  

## State of the Field

A multilingual, multicultural benchmark containing 33,000 multiple-choice questions across text and image modalities, assessing models on history-based, rule-based, and scenario-based understanding of traditional sports from 60 countries and 11 languages.
