# NewsQA
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 6 papers  

## State of the Field

Across the provided literature, NewsQA is consistently described as a measurement instrument used to evaluate question answering and machine comprehension. The dataset is composed of news articles or "news-related passages," which are characterized as being long and realistic. According to the definitions, successful performance on NewsQA requires models to engage in both "comprehension and extraction of answers" and "synthesizing information" from these passages. One paper notes the use of an "MRQA version" of the dataset for testing purposes.
