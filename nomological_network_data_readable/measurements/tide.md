# TIDE
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers

## State of the Field

The Trauma-Informed Dialogue for Empathy (TIDE) dataset, a resource of 10,000 two-turn dialogues based on 500 diverse, clinically-grounded PTSD personas, used for training and evaluating empathetic response generation.
