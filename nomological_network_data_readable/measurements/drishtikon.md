# DRISHTIKON
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  

## State of the Field

A multimodal, multilingual benchmark for evaluating cultural understanding in vision-language models, consisting of 64,288 aligned text-image multiple-choice questions across 15 Indian languages and all 36 states and union territories, covering cultural themes like festivals, attire, cuisine, and heritage.
