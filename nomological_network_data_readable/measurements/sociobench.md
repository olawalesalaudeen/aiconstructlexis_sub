# SocioBench
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  

## State of the Field

A large-scale, cross-national benchmark derived from the International Social Survey Programme (ISSP) that evaluates LLMs' ability to simulate human responses in sociological surveys across 10 domains and over 30 countries.
