**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 5 papers  

## State of the Field

IndustryOR is characterized in the provided literature as an open-source dataset of commercial Operations Research problems. One paper specifies its composition as a benchmark of "100 problems spanning linear, integer, and mixed-integer programming at various difficulty levels." The dataset's application appears to vary across studies. For instance, one line of work uses IndustryOR as the basis for developing a new benchmark focused on explainable Operations Research. In contrast, another study employs it directly as a benchmark to measure the construct of `Faithfulness`. Thus, while its core content is consistently described as a collection of real-world optimization problems, it is used both as a foundational dataset and as a direct evaluation instrument.
