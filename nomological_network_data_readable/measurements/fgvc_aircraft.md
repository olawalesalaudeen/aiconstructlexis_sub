# FGVC-Aircraft
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 4 papers  
**Also known as:** FGVC Aircraft, FGVCAircraft  

## State of the Field

Across the provided literature, FGVC-Aircraft is consistently defined as a dataset or benchmark for fine-grained visual classification of aircraft. The dataset is specified as containing images of numerous aircraft models, with one paper noting it includes "over 100 different aircraft model variants." Its most prevalent application is as a downstream dataset to evaluate model performance on fine-grained classification, and it is sometimes studied alongside other fine-grained datasets like CUB-200 and Stanford Dogs. While all sources agree on its core purpose, its specific use varies slightly. For example, one study employs it to evaluate "few-shot learning performance," whereas another uses it to assess both "visual classification and explanation quality."
