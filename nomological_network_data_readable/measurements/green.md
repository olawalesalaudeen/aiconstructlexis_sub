# GREEN
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 4 papers  

## State of the Field

Based on the provided literature, GREEN (Grounded-factual-correctness Evaluation for radioloGy reports) is an evaluation procedure that uses a finetuned Large Language Model. Its stated purpose is to assess the "factual correctness of generated radiology reports," as defined in "Noise, Adaptation, and Strategy: AssessingLLMFidelity in Decision-Making". The procedure is operationalized in the literature as a measurement for the construct of `Clinical accuracy`. It is also applied to evaluate the performance of models on the task of `Radiology report generation`. The characterization of GREEN and its application is consistent across the provided sources.
