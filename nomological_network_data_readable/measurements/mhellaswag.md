# MHELLASWAG
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 3 papers  

## State of the Field

Based on the provided data, MHELLASWAG is described as a multilingual commonsense reasoning benchmark. The single available source presents it as an instrument that "aims at collecting the multilingual understanding abilities" of models. The task format is consistently defined across the provided materials as a sentence or paragraph completion challenge. Specifically, the benchmark consists of text passages where models are required to predict the most likely continuation or ending. This operationalizes commonsense reasoning as the ability to identify the most plausible completion for a given context.
