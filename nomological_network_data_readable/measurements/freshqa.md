# FreshQA
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 5 papers  

## State of the Field

FreshQA is described in the provided literature as a question-answering dataset designed for model evaluation. According to its definition, the dataset features questions with "varying time sensitivity and complexity" and is used to assess performance with "paired correct and incorrect contexts" ("To Trust or Not to Trust? Enhancing Large Language Models' Situated Faithfulness to External Contexts"). The primary stated purpose of the instrument is to assess both "factuality and reasoning". Across the surveyed papers, FreshQA is used as a measurement tool for several different constructs. Specifically, it is reported to measure `Faithfulness`, `Compositional reasoning`, and `Generalization`.
