# Out-of-distribution evaluation
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  
**Also known as:** Out-of-Distribution Evaluation  

## State of the Field

An evaluation protocol where a model is tested on data from a different distribution than its training or fine-tuning data to assess generalization.
