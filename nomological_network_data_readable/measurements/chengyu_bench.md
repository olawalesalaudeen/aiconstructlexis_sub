# CHENGYU-BENCH
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  

## State of the Field

A comprehensive benchmark for Chinese idiom understanding, assessing models across three tasks: evaluative connotation (sentiment classification), appropriateness (misuse detection), and open cloze (idiom generation in context), with 2,937 human-verified examples covering 1,765 idioms.
