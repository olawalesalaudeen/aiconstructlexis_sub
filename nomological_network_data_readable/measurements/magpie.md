# MAGPIE
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 3 papers  

## State of the Field

The measurement instrument MAGPIE is characterized in the provided literature as a corpus of potentially idiomatic expressions, with each expression labeled as either figurative or literal. The data points to its use in evaluating how models handle non-literal language. Specifically, one paper describes using MAGPIE to test whether global affinity can distinguish between figurative and literal usage. Another paper reports adapting the dataset to measure the task of Multi-word expression identification.
