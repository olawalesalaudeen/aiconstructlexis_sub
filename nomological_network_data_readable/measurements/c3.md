## State of the Field

Across the provided literature, C3 is consistently defined as a benchmark or dataset for evaluating reading comprehension, specifically in Chinese. Its full name is given as 'Chinese Cloze-style Reading Comprehension'. The instrument is used to measure the constructs of both general 'Reading comprehension' and the more specific 'Chinese comprehension'. In addition to this primary evaluation purpose, one paper describes using C3 as a 'modern Chinese reading comprehension dataset' to test the generalizability of a procedure. This usage aligns with its consistent framing as a tool for assessing language understanding in a Chinese context.
