**Type:** Measurement

## State of the Field

A constructed dataset for evaluating hallucination detection in negated text, derived from HaluEval, BamBoo, and SelfCheckGPT-WikiBio by applying post-negation transformations and reassigning hallucination labels based on logical consistency.
