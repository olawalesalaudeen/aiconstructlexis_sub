**Type:** Measurement

## State of the Field

Across the provided literature, Traffic is consistently treated as a standard benchmark dataset containing time series data of road traffic volume records. Its most common application is to evaluate model performance on the task of time series forecasting, with one paper specifically noting its use for long-term forecasting models. Beyond general forecasting ability, the dataset is also used to measure the construct of knowledge transferability. The source snippets show that Traffic is frequently employed as part of a larger suite of time series datasets—including ETTm1, ETTm2, Electricity, and Weather—for both model evaluation and, as one paper mentions, for pre-training.
