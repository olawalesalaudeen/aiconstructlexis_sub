# RGB
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 3 papers  

## State of the Field

Based on the available data, RGB is presented as a retrieval-augmented generation benchmark. Its stated purpose is to evaluate model performance on questions that require grounding in retrieved documents, with a particular focus on assessing robustness and generalization. The benchmark is used in experimental settings to compare the accuracy of different methods, such as RPO and RAG. Within this context, it is employed alongside other datasets like PopQA, Natural Questions, and TriviaQA.
