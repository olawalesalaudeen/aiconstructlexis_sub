# EuroGEST
**Type:** Measurement  
**Referenced in:** 2 papers  

## State of the Field

A multilingual dataset for measuring gender stereotypes in 30 European languages, consisting of gendered minimal sentence pairs (EuroGESTG) and gender-neutral sentences (EuroGESTN) that can be placed in gendered templates.
